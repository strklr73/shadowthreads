Shadow Threads — Declassified System

◦ ENTRY LOGGED 04.21.2045 · SOURCE: UFO SCHEMATIC TEE — DETAILS.HTML Shadow Threads dresses an operator approaching a file. The visual system pairs a warm-neutral neumorphic canvas with classified-dossier typography: Rajdhani on everything structural, Share Tech Mono on everything data. One accent — warning orange — carries every signal the system needs to send. Everything else is grey, dashed, or dotted.

This guide extracts tokens, motifs, and components directly from UFO Schematic Tee — Details.html. Every value shown here is live on that page; nothing is approximated.
01 · Voice & ContentPROTO:OMEGA

Writes like an intelligence dossier

Impersonal, archival, faintly paranoid. The reader is an operator approaching a file. Products are subjects, dossiers, schematics, records. CTAs are protocol commands, not shopping verbs.

Casing & punctuation

  • UPPERCASE for headings, nav, CTAs, tags, chips, labels.
  • Sentence case for prose paragraphs only.
  • Middle dot · separates metadata fields.
  • Arrow → and em-dash — for transitions.
  • Single bullet character ● (U+25CF) for status dots — usually accent-colored.
  • No emoji. Anywhere. Ever.
  • No exclamation points, ellipses, or sentence-case headings.
  • No "we/you" voice. The copy is depopulated.
  • No friendly verbs: buy, shop, save, add, get.

Decorative data

Coordinates, frame counts, reference IDs, dates, clearance levels — even when they don't mean anything — sell the dossier vibe. Standard stamps pulled from the source:

Warm-neutral canvas, one accent

  • Pair #e85d26 text with #111118 or the accent glow shadow only.

03 · TypographyFONT:RAJDHANI+STM

Two families, strict division

Rajdhani carries structure — titles, nav, labels, CTAs, tags. Always uppercase, always tracked wide. Share Tech Mono carries data — body prose, prices, coordinates, tables. Tracking is part of the typography: a Rajdhani label without 0.18–0.25em spacing reads wrong.

  • Rajdhani for any structural label, tag, nav item, CTA.
  • Share Tech Mono for any paragraph, price, coordinate, field value.
  • Track Rajdhani wider as size decreases; tighten only for Display/H1.

  • Any third typeface — including italic variants of either.
04 · NeumorphismMOTIF:01

Raised, inset, flat

Every interactive surface is either raised (resting) or inset (selected / pressed / contained). Selection adds a 1px accent outline on top of the inset. Paired shadows — cool-shadow bottom-right, white highlight top-left — sit on top of the warm-neutral canvas. No hard borders.

State transitions

  • Resting → hover: translateY(-2 to -5px) + deeper raised shadow.
  • Resting → active (selected): swap raised → inset + 1px accent outline inside.
  • Button press: swap raised → inset + scale(0.98). Duration 0.35s, ease-out.
  • Image wells, spec sheets, and qty boxes are always inset — they contain data.
  • No solid backgrounds or borders to communicate active state. Shadow + outline only.
  • No drop-shadows with blur > 20px or offset > 8px. Break the surface illusion.
05 · Blueprint MotifsMOTIF:02

Schematic overlay, over neumorphism

Where neumorphism handles surface, blueprint handles meaning. Corner marks frame inspection zones. Dashed dividers separate fields. The scan sweep moves continuously across image panels. A 16×16 grid lives inside recessed panels at 8% opacity.

Grid recess (inset panel background)

● GRID · 16×16 · 8%
  • Corner marks (4 orange L-brackets, 1px, 18×18) frame any image canvas.
  • Scan sweep: 2px tall gradient line, 5s linear infinite, top→bottom. Accent fades at edges.
  • Dashed dividers at rgba(138,138,154,0.25) or stroke-dasharray="3 3".
  • Crosshair on image hover: 2 orange 1px lines following cursor + redacted black X/Y readout.
  • Schematic SVG: 1px stroke blueprint-line, 1–2 orange dot highlights max.
  • Don't stack multiple sweeps or more than one crosshair on a single surface.
  • No filled illustrations or gradients inside schematic SVG — stroke only.
06 · IconographySET:INLINE

Intentionally sparse, 1–2px stroke

The product ships almost no icons — that's the point. What exists is drawn inline: 1–2px stroke SVG, no fill, currentColor, 12–18px. The ● character substitutes for status glyphs. If a new utility icon is needed, use Lucide at 1.5–2px stroke as the nearest visual match — and flag the substitution.

08 · MotionEASE:CB-0.4.0.2.1

Instrument-like, never playful

One easing curve, three durations, a handful of signature loops. Nothing bounces, nothing rotates for flair, nothing springs. Motion conveys surveillance and measurement.

TokenDurationUsed forPreview
--dur-fast0.25sHover color, icon state, link underline
--dur-normal0.35sButton press, shadow swap, tab change
--dur-slow0.45sOverlay / toast transitions
scan sweep5s linearImage panel scan line, hero
pulse-dot2s infiniteStatus indicator glow

Easing is cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) on everything that isn't a linear loop.

  • Signature loops: scan sweep (4–5s), pulse-dot (2s), arc-spin for HUD rings (6s), flicker (3s opacity 1→0.5→0.9→1).
  • State changes are measured — 0.25s on hovers, 0.35s on selections, 0.45s for overlays.
  • No bounces, no springs, no rotation as decoration.
  • No durations under 0.15s or over 0.6s for state changes.

13 · FeedbackCOMP:TOAST+LOCK

Toast, restricted overlay

Toasts are redacted-black pills with a 1px accent border, a pulsing dot, and Rajdhani body text tracked at 0.2em. They slide up from the bottom over 0.45s. 14 · LayoutGRID:DETAIL

1400px max, three-column detail

Content caps at 1400px with 2rem horizontal gutters. The detail page layout is a three-column grid — an 80px thumb rail, a fluid image, a 460px info panel — collapsing to 2 cols at 1150px and stacking at 860px.
